GAP The Series

GAP The Series is a girls love type of series, also known as “Yuri”. It is based on the fiction GAP: Pink Theory, which is one of the most popular fictions on the website Dek-D.com, by the author Jao Pla Noi. This series features 2 brand new actresses, Freen Sarocha Chankimha as Sam and Becky Rebecca Patricia Armstrong as Mond. GAP The Series is produced by Idol Factory, with Saint Suppapong as the producer and director. GAP The Series tells the story of Mond (Becky Rebecca), who idolizes Sam (Freen Sarocha), a royal descendant who is perfect in both her appearance and wealth. Mond has always considered Sam her role model since she was a child. When Mond graduated, she decided to apply for a job at Sam's company. The more she got closer to Sam, her feelings gradually changed from idolizing to love. Mond almost gave up due to the many obstacles, such as the 8-year age gap, the social status differences, and this new-found feeling that Mond had for women. However, things changed when she realized that Sam felt the same way.
