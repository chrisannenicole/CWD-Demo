Synopsis

From the age of 17 to 21, Minji and Yuna have been each other's best friends. Since one day, the relationship has subtly changed. Min-hee's crazy dream about Yuna begins! Their relationship began to become something other than best friends. Unrequited love, excitement, obsession. "'Bad Ideas' are growing in their own minds. Where do their bad ideas settle on a lush college campus?
